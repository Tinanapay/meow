ABOUT SATO

Sato is a smart mobile app that transforms your phone into a real-time learning tool for electronics. With advanced object recognition, Sato instantly identifies components like resistors, capacitors, diodes, transistors, and more — all through your camera. No manuals, no guesswork. Whether you're a student, hobbyist, or professional, Sato delivers clear insights on how each component works, where it's used, and why it matters. Just point, scan, and learn — anytime, anywhere.

Features

  • Instant information on electronic components
  • Real-Time Component Detection Identify resistors, capacitors, diodes, transistors & ICs instantly using your phone camera.
  • Made for All Skill Levels Clean, intuitive UI for students, makers, and pros alike.
  • Smarter Circuit Exploration Accelerate hands-on learning and prototyping with intelligent component insights.
  • Broad Device Compatibility Works seamlessly on most smartphones — no special hardware needed.
